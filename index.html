What We Do
The Game Development industry is an amalgamation of many different industries that (the former) has grown rapidly over the years. Yet, with it being the dominant entertainment industry, it becomes increasingly difficult to pursue a career as a game developer. While AAA (Triple A) studios are determined to make as much money as possible, it's important to remind people why games were made in the first place. The Game Development Club plans to serve this goal as we create games with the focus of being fun.
We create design docs to facilitate our programming, designing, and overall phases of development. Tools such as Trello, Blender, Unity, Clip Studio Paint, and BandLab all make it possible for us to make games.
